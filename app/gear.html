My gear

What's in my backpack

As I started training for triathlon in late 2015, I thought it could be interesting to add a section with the gear that I use.

Swimming

  • Arena swimsuit, goggles, and swim cap
    Basic equipment for swimming pool. I'm not doing any openwater yet.

Bike

  • Scott Sportster X40
    Urban bike to ride in the city, and the occasional trail in Casa de Campo. I want to buy a road bike for the next season, I'm already considering a Scott Speedster 20 or a Canyon Endurace AL 6.0.

  • Gore Bike Wear Element Bib Tights
    Very confortable and warm bib tights, the quality makes the difference. — Amazon

  • Gore Bike Wear Element Windstopper Soft Shell Jacket
    I love this soft shell jacket. It fits perfectly and protects from wind, and light rain. — Amazon

  • Endura Xtract long sleeve
    I usually wear this under the jacket in cold weather. — Chain Reaction Cycles

  • Cold weather gloves
    5€ pair of gloves from Decathlon.

Running

  • Running t-shirts
    Both long and short sleeve t-shirts from races, depending on the temperature, and time of the year.

  • Kalenji running tights
    I just have one pair but they wash and dry very quickly. I have had them for years.

Mountaineering

  • Bestard Advance K Pro
    My first rigid boots, highly recommended. — Barrabes

  • The North Face Summit Series jacket
    I use it almost every day, and in the mountains. It's quite waterproof, but if it starts raining/snowing I prefer to wear a ski jacket.

  • The North Face Jester / Terra 65
    I like to carry a small backbpack for short trips. For longer excursions I use a big 65L backpack. — Barrabes

I've bought the previous products online from Amazon, Wiggle, Chain Reaction Cycles, Decathlon, and Barrabes, and I'm very happy so far with the products and the shipping.
